DJ Web App!
To swipe between songs: Move your left wrist to the left to go to the previous song, and move your right wrist to the right to go to the next song.
WARNING: If there are no next / previous songs, there is a chance the site may bug, and / or start lagging, possibly crashing your chrome.
Please do not move to a "previous song" if there is no previous song, and vice versa.
